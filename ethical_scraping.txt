Purpose of robots.txt and Ethical Scraping:
The robots.txt file is used by websites to define rules for web crawlers, restricting access to certain parts of the site. This prevents server overload, protects sensitive information, and ensures ethical data scraping practices. By respecting these rules, web scrapers can operate legally and responsibly, without violating a site's terms of service.
